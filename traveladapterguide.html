Plug & Socket Types

There are twelve different types of power plugs and corresponding sockets used around the world. Why are there so many different standards? The simple answer is that international travel was not nearly as common when electricity came of age as it is today. The father's of the early electrical grid were not working together on how to make a lamp in the UK compatible with sockets in the US and the idea of portable electronic devices was still many decades away.

The most common plug types most travellers need to be familar with are Type A, C, F, and N.
