Highest Rated Travel Adapters of 2019

Not sure what travel adapter to get for your next trip? See our power converter reviews based on consumer ratings, reliability, and compatibility in over 200 countries.

The first two adapters on our list (Foval and Bestek) will convert voltage no matter what device you use. The second two adapters on our list (Iron-M and Urophylla) are less expensive and will allow you to use a US Type A plug in almost every country but will not convert the voltage like option #1 and #2.
