Do you need a power adapter when travelling to Italy from the United States? Yes, for most devices you will need a plug adapter and power converter while in Italy. Italy uses the Type L electrical plug and socket.

Type C and Type F power plugs are also generally compatible with Italian electrical sockets. The standard voltage is 230V and standard frequency is 50Hz. You will need a power converter when visiting from the United States.
