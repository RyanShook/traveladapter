Are you travelling to France soon and need to know what kind of power adapter to use? France uses the TYPE E electrical plug and socket. Type C, Type F, and Type N power plugs are also generally compatible with France's electrical sockets. The standard voltage is 230 V and standard frequency is 50 Hz. You will need a power converter when visiting from the United States to step down the voltage.
