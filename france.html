Do you need a power adapter when travelling to France from the United States? Yes, for most devices you will need a plug adapter and power converter while in France. France uses the TYPE E electrical plug and socket.

Type C, and Type F power plugs are also generally compatible with French electrical sockets. The standard voltage is 230 V and standard frequency is 50 Hz. You will need a power converter when visiting from the United States to step down the voltage.
